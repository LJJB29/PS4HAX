GoldHEN PS4 Modding Guide

Introduction

GoldHEN is a Homebrew Enabler for the PlayStation 4, allowing users to run unsigned code and customize their console experience. Developed by the community, it offers a range of features to enhance your PS4's capabilities.

Prerequisites

  • A PlayStation 4 console running firmware version 9.00 or lower. Check firmware compatibility.
  • A USB drive formatted to exFAT or FAT32.
  • GoldHEN payload files. Download from the official GitHub repository.

Installation Guide

  1. Prepare the USB Drive:
    • Format your USB drive to exFAT or FAT32.
    • Create a folder named PS4 at the root of the drive.
    • Within the PS4 folder, create another folder named EXPLOIT.
    • Place the GoldHEN payload file into the EXPLOIT folder.
  2. Launch the PS4 Exploit:
    • Connect the USB drive to your PS4.
    • Navigate to the browser and visit a trusted exploit hosting site compatible with your firmware version.
    • Select the appropriate exploit to inject the GoldHEN payload.
  3. Activate GoldHEN:
    • Once the exploit is executed, GoldHEN should load automatically.
    • Confirm its activation by checking for the GoldHEN icon or notifications.

FAQs

Is jailbreaking my PS4 legal? Jailbreaking may void your warranty and violate Sony's terms of service. Ensure you're aware of the legal implications in your region.

Can I revert my PS4 to its original state? While some users have reported success in restoring their PS4 to factory settings, there's no guaranteed method to reverse a jailbreak.

Will I still receive official updates? After jailbreaking, it's advisable to avoid official updates, as they may patch the exploit and remove jailbreak functionalities.
